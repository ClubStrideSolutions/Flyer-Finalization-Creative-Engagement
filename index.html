20-Minute Sprint Instructions

  1. Minutes 1-10: Final Edits
    • Check all phone numbers and addresses one last time
    • Verify QR codes work (test with your phone)
    • Make sure text is readable (no tiny fonts!)
    • Confirm colors print well (not too light)
  2. Minutes 11-15: Partner Check
    • Trade flyers with another team
    • Look for: typos, unclear information, missing details
    • Give specific feedback: "The phone number is hard to read" not "It looks weird"
  3. Minutes 16-20: Submit for Printing
    • Make final corrections based on feedback
    • Save as PDF (best for printing)
    • Share the link to your graphic in the team chat
    • Fill out print request form (how many copies needed)

What's a 30-Second Pitch?

A quick, natural way to introduce your flyer that covers:

  • Hook: Get their attention (5 seconds)
  • Value: Why they should care (10 seconds)
  • Details: Key info from flyer (10 seconds)
  • Action: What to do next (5 seconds)

Demo Instructions

Part 1: Pitch Prep (10 minutes)

Example Pitch Structure:
"Hey! (show flyer) Real quick ‚Äì did you know you can get free STI testing without your parents knowing? (pause) This shows all the places in Vallejo where it's totally confidential and free. They even have Spanish speakers. (point to QR code) Scan this to find the closest one. Keep it in case you or a friend needs it!"
  • Write your pitch using the structure above
  • Practice with your team 2-3 times
  • Time yourself ‚Äì aim for 25-35 seconds
  • Make it sound like YOU, not a robot!

Part 2: Group Demos (15 minutes)

  • Each team presents their pitch to the full group
  • Hold up your flyer so everyone can see
  • Speak clearly but conversationally
  • It's okay to be nervous ‚Äì we're all learning!

30-Minute Creation & Practice Session

  1. Choose Your Platform (5 minutes)
    • Pick the ONE platform your team uses most/best
    • Consider your comfort level with creating content
    • Think about which platform your school peers actually use
    • Stick with what you know - don't try something totally new!
  2. Prepare Your Materials (10 minutes)
    • Adapt your flyer for your chosen platform (crop, resize, etc.)
    • Write out your key messages or script
    • Gather any additional materials (songs, props, etc.)
    • Set up your phone/device and test the platform
  3. Create Your Content (10 minutes)
    • Make your Instagram polls, Snapchat content, or TikTok video
    • Don't aim for perfection - aim for authentic and clear
    • Save drafts but don't post yet (save for Monday launch!)
    • Take screenshots of your content to show the group
  4. Practice & Get Feedback (5 minutes)
    • Show your content to another team
    • Ask: Is the message clear? Would you share this?
    • Make quick adjustments based on feedback
    • Plan when you'll post during launch week
